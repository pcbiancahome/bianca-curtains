About Us

Bianca Home —
Trusted Since 1980

For over 45 years, Bianca has shaped the way India decorates its homes—blending craftsmanship, innovation, and design to create premium home textiles that elevate everyday living into a lifestyle.

Our thoughtfully crafted range of home decor products ensures effortless choices and instant transformations, making us a trusted authority in home styling. Available across 2,500+ outlets nationwide—including two flagship stores—Bianca is more than a brand—it’s a legacy.

With Bianca Curtains, we've elevated the custom curtain journey—from curated fabric collections and expert consultations to precision craftsmanship and professional installation, we deliver a truly premium experience from start to finish.
